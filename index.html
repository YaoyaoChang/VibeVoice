Abstract

We present VibeVoice, a neural TTS system designed to preserve prosody and vibe across diverse speaking styles, while remaining robust to long inputs and noisy punctuation. Compared to baselines (e.g., Gemini TTS, ElevenLabs), VibeVoice aims to produce more stable phrasing, consistent timbre, and better pause planning for downstream alignment tasks. We release audio demos and prompts for reproducibility.
